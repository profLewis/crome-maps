EuroCrops V2

Executive Summary

Harmonized EU-wide crop parcel dataset covering 18 countries from 2008-2023, with crop types mapped to a unified HCAT v4 taxonomy. Contains millions of individual field parcels derived from national LPIS/IACS declarations. V2 significantly expands V1 coverage (16 → 18 countries, 2008-2023 vs 2018-2021).

Description

EuroCrops V2 combines self-declared crop reporting datasets from EU member states into a harmonized pan-European dataset. Each parcel includes the original national crop code, translated to the Hierarchical Crop and Agriculture Taxonomy (HCAT v4). Countries included: AT, BE (Flanders/Wallonia), BG, CZ, DE (Brandenburg/NRW), DK, EE, ES, FI, FR, IE, IT (Tuscany), NL, PT, SI, SK.

Provenance

Created by the Joint Research Centre (JRC) of the European Commission, building on the original EuroCrops project by TU Munich. Source data from national LPIS/IACS databases. HCAT v4 taxonomy provides cross-country harmonization of crop types.

Classification System

HCAT v4 (Hierarchical Crop and Agriculture Taxonomy) — 4 levels with ~200 crop types

Accuracy Assessment

Based on official LPIS/IACS declarations — these are self-reported by farmers and verified by national agencies. Accuracy of crop type attribution is generally high (>95%) for major crops, as declarations are tied to EU subsidy payments. Geometric accuracy depends on the national LPIS system.
